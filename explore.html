Take a look at all of our flowers and gardens

The botanic garden will take your breath away and give you a new meaning of beauty in nature. We have many species of flowers that bloom semi-annually, and annually. There are plenty of things to do and explore as you gain more knowledge of the flowers that encompass the world and the west coast region of the United States. We are planning on receiving more plants by next season including the fabled Corpse Flower

So come visit us and let us enlighten you with more knowledge of the science of plants. We have experts on hand that care for the flowers day in and day out giving special care to them to make sure they live for such a long time generations pass.
